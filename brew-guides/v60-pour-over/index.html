Baseline V60

Coffee: 18g medium-fine

Water: 288g at 92-96C

Ratio: 1:16

Total time: 2:45 to 3:30

Pour structure

Bloom

Pour 45g water and wait 30 to 45 seconds. Swirl gently if the bed is dry.

Main pour

Pour to 180g in slow circles. Keep the stream steady and avoid the paper wall.

Finish

Pour to 288g, swirl once, and let the bed draw down flat.

Adjust by taste

Sour

Grind finer, use hotter water, or extend the drawdown slightly.

Bitter

Grind coarser, reduce agitation, or use a lower temperature.

Flat

Try fresher beans, a cleaner grinder, or a recipe with more extraction.
