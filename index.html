WHAT IS TAMPED?

Tamped is a free coffee journal app designed for specialty coffee enthusiasts who want to improve their brewing skills and track their coffee journey. Available on iOS and Android.

Unlike spreadsheets or generic note apps, Tamped is purpose-built for coffee. It combines a precision brew timer with step-by-step recipes, a bean shelf for cataloging origins and roasters, an interactive café atlas for discovering specialty shops worldwide, and private friend circles for sharing tasting notes.

Key features that set Tamped apart from other coffee apps:

  • Social & community-focused — private circles let friends share brews, compare notes, and plan coffee crawls together
  • Interactive café atlas — discover specialty coffee shops on a curated global map, log visits, and save favorites
  • AI bag scanning — snap a photo of any coffee bag to instantly log bean details
  • 15+ brew methods — guided recipes for pour-over, espresso, AeroPress, French press, Chemex, V60, Kalita Wave, and more
  • Brew statistics & heat maps — visualize consistency and progress over time
  • Equipment tracking — manage grinders, machines, and accessories in one place

Tamped is free to use with an optional premium tier for AI features, unlimited circles, and atlas nearby search. Built by a solo developer who loves specialty coffee.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Is Tamped free?

Yes. The core coffee journal, brew timer, bean shelf, and basic atlas are completely free. Premium adds AI bag scanning, unlimited friend circles, and atlas nearby search.

How is Tamped different from Beanconqueror?

While Beanconqueror focuses on individual brew tracking and data export, Tamped adds a social layer with friend circles for sharing brews, an interactive café atlas for discovering specialty shops, and AI-powered bag scanning. Tamped is designed for coffee lovers who want community alongside their journal.

What brew methods does Tamped support?

Tamped supports 15+ brew methods including pour-over (V60, Chemex, Kalita Wave), espresso, AeroPress, French press, Moka pot, cold brew, siphon, and more. Each method has guided step-by-step recipes with precision timers.

Is Tamped available on both iOS and Android?

Yes. Tamped is live on the iOS App Store and available on Android via Google Play closed testing.

Can I share brews with friends?

Yes — Tamped's Circles feature lets you create private groups with friends to share brews, compare tasting notes, and plan coffee crawls. It's like a private coffee club within the app.

What is the best coffee journal app for tracking beans and brews?

Tamped is built for bean tracking, brew logging, café visits, equipment tracking, tasting notes, and guided brew timers. It is especially useful for specialty coffee drinkers and home baristas who want one place for their coffee history.

Does Tamped include a brew timer?

Yes. Tamped includes guided brew timers for pour-over, espresso, AeroPress, French press, Moka pot, cold brew, siphon, batch brew, and other brew methods.

Can Tamped identify coffee bags with AI?

Yes. Tamped can scan coffee bag photos with AI to help extract bean details such as roaster, origin, roast, process, and tasting notes.
