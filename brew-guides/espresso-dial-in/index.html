Starter recipe

Dose: 18g

Yield: 36g

Time: 25 to 32 seconds

Decision: adjust by taste, not by time alone.

Dial-in sequence

1. Lock dose

Pick a dose that fits your basket. Keep it steady until the shot tastes close.

2. Set yield

Use 1:2 as a baseline. Go shorter for body, longer for clarity.

3. Change grind

If it runs fast and sour, grind finer. If it runs slow and dry, grind coarser.

Espresso troubleshooting

Sour and thin

Try finer grind, hotter water, or a slightly longer yield.

Bitter and dry

Try coarser grind, lower yield, or reduce contact time.

Good once, lost later

Log the grinder setting, dose, yield, and time so the next shot has a reference.
