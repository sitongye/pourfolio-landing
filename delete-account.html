Delete Your Account

To delete your Tamped account and all associated data, open the app and follow these steps:

  1. Open the Settings tab (gear icon)
  2. Scroll to the bottom of the Settings page
  3. Tap "Delete account"
  4. Confirm by typing DELETE in the dialog

What gets deleted

  • Your profile and account information
  • All brew logs, beans, recipes, and gadgets
  • Café visits and wishlist items
  • Saved places and atlas collections
  • Circle memberships
  • Your Firebase authentication credentials

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Your data will be removed immediately upon confirmation.
